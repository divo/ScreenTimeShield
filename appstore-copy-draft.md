## Description

Screen Time limits can be bypassed in 10 seconds. Unplug can't.

Set a schedule. Pick your apps. When the block is active, there's no override, no passcode workaround, no "just 15 more minutes." The apps are locked until your time is up.

HOW IT WORKS
Choose which apps and websites to block. Set your start and end time. Unplug locks them on schedule — no willpower required.

WHY IT'S DIFFERENT
Native Screen Time lets you tap "Ignore Limit" and you're back to scrolling. Unplug uses Apple's managed device framework to enforce restrictions that can't be dismissed. There's no backdoor.

WHAT YOU GET
· Unskippable app and website blocking on a daily schedule
· Quick-block: restrict apps for the next hour with one tap
· Refocus notifications when you open restricted apps outside blocked hours
· Works with any app or website
· No account required, no data collected

One-time purchase. No subscription. No ads.

Unplug — Don't just set limits. Lock them.
