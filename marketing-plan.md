## Phase 2 — Organic Content (free, high ROI)

### Content Angle
You are the story. Someone who built a tool because they had a real problem with their own phone. Authentic, first-person content outperforms ads in this niche.

### Formats to Make
1. **"I built an unskippable app blocker — here's what happened"**
   - Your own experience using it. 30-day angle. Hero piece.

2. **"Every other app blocker has this loophole"**
   - Show Screen Time being bypassed in 5 seconds, then show Unplug not budging.
   - Contrast content — very shareable.

3. **"How I actually stopped doomscrolling after 10pm"**
   - Lifestyle angle, softer, broader audience reach.

### Where to Publish
- TikTok + Instagram Reels (same video, both platforms)
- Reddit: r/nosurf, r/digitalminimalism, r/productivity — genuine posts, not ads
- Twitter/X: productivity audience is active here

### Cadence
3–4 pieces of content → see what lands → double down on what works
